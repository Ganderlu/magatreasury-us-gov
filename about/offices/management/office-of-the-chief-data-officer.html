Office of the Chief Data Officer

The Office of the Chief Data Officer (CDO) is commonly associated with improving data governance, quality, and accessibility. It supports the use of data to improve decision-making, transparency, and operational effectiveness.

Common Responsibilities

  • Data governance: standards, definitions, and stewardship roles.
  • Data quality: consistency, validation, and documentation.
  • Open data: improving public access to datasets and metadata.
  • Privacy and security: responsible use and protection of sensitive information.
