Public Affairs

The Office of Public Affairs supports communication between Treasury and the public. The office helps explain policy actions, provides timely information, and supports transparency through press engagement and public-facing content.

What This Office Does

  • Coordinates press and public communications across Treasury offices.
  • Publishes announcements, statements, and other public information as appropriate.
  • Supports clear messaging during major policy events and operational updates.

Typical Activities

  • Media engagement: supporting press inquiries and briefings.
  • Public information: maintaining web updates and releases.
  • Messaging coordination: aligning communication across offices.
  • Crisis communications: supporting rapid, accurate updates when needed.

How It Supports the Public

Clear communication helps people understand government actions and find reliable information. Public Affairs supports transparency and access to timely updates.

  • Improves clarity of public announcements and policy summaries.
  • Helps ensure information is consistent and easy to find.
  • Supports public trust through accurate, timely communication.
