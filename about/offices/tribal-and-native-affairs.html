Tribal and Native Affairs

Tribal and Native Affairs supports engagement and coordination on issues that affect Tribal Nations and Native communities. The work helps ensure communication is respectful, consistent, and responsive, and that relevant programs and policies are implemented with appropriate outreach and understanding.

What This Office Does

  • Serves as a coordination point for Tribal consultation and outreach efforts related to Treasury programs and policies.
  • Helps connect Treasury offices with Tribal leaders and community stakeholders to support constructive engagement.
  • Supports clear public information about relevant programs, resources, and opportunities where applicable.

How Engagement Often Works

  • Listening sessions: gathering input and feedback from communities.
  • Coordination: aligning program offices to respond consistently to questions and needs.
  • Program guidance: helping ensure communications are clear and accessible.

How It Supports the Public

Strong engagement can help improve program accessibility, increase awareness, and support effective implementation of services that reach Tribal Nations and Native communities.

  • Promotes clear communication and responsive coordination.
  • Supports consistent outreach across programs.
  • Helps improve access to accurate information and resources.
