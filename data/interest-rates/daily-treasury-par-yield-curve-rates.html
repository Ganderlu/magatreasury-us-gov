Daily Treasury Par Yield Curve Rates

“Par yield curve rates” are yields for Treasury securities by maturity (for example, 1 month, 3 months, 2 years, 10 years). They are commonly used to describe the Treasury yield curve and are a reference point for pricing and risk management across financial markets.

What This Data Represents

  • Daily series: a daily snapshot of yields across maturities.
  • Par yields: yields implied for securities priced near par value.
  • Curve shape: helps show steepening/flattening over time.

How People Use It

  • Benchmarking: compare borrowing costs across maturities.
  • Trend analysis: track how rates move with inflation and policy expectations.
  • Modeling: discount rates, forward rates, and term structure analysis.
