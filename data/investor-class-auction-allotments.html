Investor Class Auction Allotments

Investor class auction allotments summarize how Treasury auction awards are distributed across bidder categories. This information is used to understand demand composition and how different investor types participate in auctions.

What “Allotment” Means

  • Awarded amount: the portion of an auction allocated to a class of bidders.
  • Participation mix: how demand is split across investor types.
  • Market context: distribution can shift with rates, risk appetite, and liquidity needs.

How People Use This Data

  • Demand analysis: compare participation across auctions and maturities.
  • Trend monitoring: identify shifts in buyer base over time.
  • Research: study auction outcomes and market structure.
