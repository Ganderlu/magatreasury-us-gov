Press Contacts

This page provides a structured way for members of the media to identify the right contact pathway. It also outlines the information that helps a request get routed quickly and accurately.

What to Include in a Media Inquiry

  • Full name, outlet, and preferred contact method
  • Topic and deadline (with time zone)
  • Specific questions and relevant background
  • Any reference numbers or links to related releases

Common Topics

  • Press releases and statements
  • Sanctions actions and compliance guidance
  • Financial markets and economic policy announcements
  • Program updates and public events
