Treasury Targets Oil Network Generating Hundreds of Millions of Dollars for Iran’s Military

February 8, 2025

This story provides plain-language context for how illicit finance networks are described in Treasury communications and how targeted actions can disrupt revenue streams supporting prohibited activity.

What “Targets” Typically Means

  • Identifying people and entities connected to the activity described
  • Using legal authorities to restrict certain transactions and access
  • Publishing information to support compliance by financial institutions

Why Oil Networks Are Discussed

  • Revenue flows: illicit sales can generate funds that support sanctioned activity.
  • Financial intermediaries: networks often rely on front companies and facilitators.
  • Compliance impact: designations influence screening and risk management.
