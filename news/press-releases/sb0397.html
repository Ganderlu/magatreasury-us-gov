Treasury Issues Interim CAMT Guidance to Reduce Burdens, Support U.S. Investment, and Maximize Growth

Release ID: SB0397 • May 16, 2026

This page provides a high-level explanation of what “interim guidance” typically means in a tax context and how stakeholders generally use such guidance to plan compliance and implementation steps.

What “Interim Guidance” Usually Means

  • Clarifies definitions, calculations, and compliance expectations
  • Provides practical steps until a longer-form rulemaking is finalized
  • Helps reduce uncertainty for taxpayers and practitioners

How People Use Guidance Like This

  • Planning: evaluate impact and adjust processes and reporting.
  • Implementation: update internal controls and documentation.
  • Questions: identify areas where additional clarification may be needed.
