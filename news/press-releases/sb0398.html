Secretary Bessent Announces 2026 G20 Finance Track Agenda and Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors Meeting in Asheville, North Carolina

Release ID: SB0398 • May 19, 2026

This page provides a plain-language overview of a leadership announcement related to the G20 Finance Track. It outlines what the “Finance Track” generally covers and why Treasury communications may preview agenda themes in advance of meetings.

What the G20 Finance Track Typically Covers

  • Global economic conditions and coordinated policy priorities
  • Financial stability and market functioning
  • International tax and financial architecture topics
  • Issues affecting growth, inflation, and cross-border investment

Why Meeting Announcements Matter

  • Transparency: communicates agenda themes and priorities to the public.
  • Coordination: signals the topics under discussion for policy stakeholders.
  • Planning: helps media and participants align on dates and locations.
