Webcasts

Webcasts are used for public meetings, announcements, and events that are streamed live or available for replay. This page provides a simple guide for what to expect and how to prepare.

Before You Watch

  • Confirm start time and time zone for live events.
  • Use a modern browser and a stable internet connection.
  • If captions are available, enable them for accessibility.

What Webcast Pages Usually Include

  • Event title, time, and agenda
  • Streaming link and replay information
  • Supporting documents or related releases
