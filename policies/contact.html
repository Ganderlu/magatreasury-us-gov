Need help with an order, shipping, refunds, or payment questions? Use the details below. For the fastest resolution, include your order number and the email used at checkout.

Contact Form

If your browser supports it, you can send us a message using your email application. Fill out the form and click “Send Message” to open an email draft with your details.

If your device does not support mailto links, email us directly at support@ourmagashop.com.

Support Details

Email: support@ourmagashop.com

What to include:

  • Order number
  • Email used at checkout
  • Item name(s)
  • Shipping address confirmation (if address-related)
  • Photos for damaged/incorrect items

We respond as quickly as possible. During high-volume periods, replies may take longer, but every request is handled in the order received.

Common Questions

Where is my order? Check your tracking link first (if provided). If tracking hasn’t updated for an extended period, send us your order number and we’ll help.

I entered the wrong address. Contact us immediately. If fulfillment has not started, we may be able to update it. If shipped, changes may not be possible.

I need a refund or replacement. Please include photos if the item arrived damaged or incorrect. Review our Refund Policy for details.
