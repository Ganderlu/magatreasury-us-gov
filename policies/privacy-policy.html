Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Our Maga Shop collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you visit our website or place an order. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

1) Information We Collect

We collect information to process orders, provide customer service, maintain security, and improve the shopping experience.

  • Contact information: such as your email address and phone number (when provided).
  • Order and shipping information: such as name, shipping address, and order details.
  • Payment-related details: payment status confirmations, transaction identifiers, and invoice/order references from payment providers. We do not store full card details on our servers.
  • Device and usage data: such as browser type, IP address, and pages visited, used for analytics and security.

2) How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Sending transactional messages (order confirmations and updates).
  • Providing customer support and handling returns/refunds.
  • Fraud prevention, risk checks, and security monitoring.
  • Improving website functionality and user experience.

3) Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working, store preferences, and understand how visitors use our pages. Cookies may be used for analytics, site performance, and security.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain functionality, including checkout or cart features.

4) Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate the store and deliver services. We do not sell your personal information.

  • Payment providers: to create invoices, validate payment, and confirm payment status.
  • Shipping carriers: to deliver orders and provide tracking.
  • Fraud prevention and security vendors: to protect the platform and customers.
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety.

5) Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including recordkeeping, dispute resolution, security, and legal obligations. Retention periods depend on the type of data and the purpose for which it was collected.

6) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. No online system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we use safeguards aligned with industry practices.

7) Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing or request restrictions.

To request access or changes, contact us using the information on the Contact page and include your order number when applicable.

8) Communications

We may send you messages that are necessary to complete your purchase and provide support. If marketing communications are offered, you can opt out at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the message.

9) International Visitors

If you access the site from outside the country where our operations are based, your information may be processed in a different jurisdiction that may have different data protection laws. We apply safeguards appropriate to the services we provide.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates are effective when posted on this page. If changes are significant, we may provide additional notice.

Tip: For faster support, include your order ID and the email used at checkout when contacting us. If you are referencing a payment invoice, include the invoice/order reference shown on the payment page.
