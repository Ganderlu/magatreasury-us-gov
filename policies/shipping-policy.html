Shipping Policy

This Shipping Policy describes how orders are processed, shipped, and delivered. Shipping timelines may vary depending on product type, stock availability, destination, and carrier conditions.

1) Order Processing

Orders are typically processed after payment confirmation. Processing may include verification, packing, label creation, and carrier handoff. Please ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout. If you need an update, contact support with your order number.

Verification

Some orders may require additional verification to prevent fraud. This may include confirmation of billing details or payment validation.

Fulfillment

Fulfillment begins once an order is queued. High-demand periods can extend fulfillment time due to volume and carrier constraints.

2) Shipping Carriers and Tracking

We work with common carriers to deliver orders safely. When available, a tracking number is provided after shipment. Tracking updates may take 24–48 hours to appear depending on the carrier scan cadence.

  • Tracking shows the carrier’s most recent scan event, not real-time GPS.
  • “Label created” means the label is generated; the package may still be awaiting carrier pickup or a first scan.
  • If tracking appears stuck, it may resume after the next facility scan.

3) Estimated Delivery Times

Delivery estimates are not guarantees. Carrier delays, weather events, holidays, and local service disruptions can affect arrival times. We will assist with investigations for significantly delayed shipments when appropriate.

  • Domestic shipments often arrive faster than international shipments.
  • Rural or remote areas may require extra transit time.
  • Customs processing (international) can add additional time.

4) Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing accurate address information. Incorrect or incomplete addresses can cause delivery failures, returns, or additional carrier fees. If an order is returned due to address issues, we may require updated address confirmation and reshipment costs.

5) Missing, Lost, or Stolen Packages

If your package is missing or marked delivered but not received, contact us promptly. We may request additional information and may initiate a carrier claim or investigation depending on the situation.

  • If marked delivered, check with neighbors, mailroom staff, and nearby safe locations.
  • In some cases, carriers require a waiting period before a claim can be filed.

6) Partial Shipments and Multiple Packages

Certain orders may ship in multiple packages. If you receive part of your order, please allow additional time for remaining packages to arrive. Each package may have separate tracking information.

7) International Shipping (if available)

International shipments may be subject to customs inspections and import duties/taxes. These fees are set by the destination country and are the customer’s responsibility unless explicitly stated otherwise.
