COVID19 Economic Relief

This section provides an overview of COVID-19 economic relief topics and how major categories of support are commonly organized. Use the links below to view information for families and workers, small businesses, state/local/tribal governments, and American industry.

How Relief Is Typically Structured

  • Direct support: benefits or payments intended to help households meet immediate needs.
  • Stabilization: programs that help businesses and markets maintain continuity and employment.
  • Public-sector support: funding and flexibility for state, local, and tribal governments to respond and recover.
  • Accountability: reporting, oversight, and controls to help ensure programs operate responsibly.
