Policy Issues • COVID19 Economic Relief

State, Local, and Tribal Governments

Public-sector COVID-19 relief generally supports response and recovery activities, continuity of essential services, and stabilization of budgets impacted by emergency conditions. Programs may include formula funding, targeted grants, and reporting guidance for eligible uses.

Common Uses of Funds (Examples)

  • Public health response and emergency operations
  • Community support services and continuity planning
  • Economic recovery efforts and assistance programs
  • Infrastructure and operational improvements where authorized
  • Administrative capacity for compliance and reporting

Planning and Compliance

Public-sector programs often require clear documentation of how funds are used, procurement practices, and reporting of outcomes. This supports transparency and responsible stewardship.

Typical Reporting Expectations

  • Tracking of expenditures by category and period
  • Documentation supporting eligibility and allowable use
  • Procurement records and vendor documentation
  • Performance or outcome summaries when required
  • Retention of records for oversight and audit purposes

Coordination and Implementation

Implementation can involve coordination across departments, agencies, and partners. Many jurisdictions establish dedicated teams to manage guidance, oversight, and program execution.

  • Assign clear owners for compliance and reporting.
  • Create internal guidance for consistent decision-making.
  • Use standard templates for documentation and approvals.
  • Perform periodic internal reviews to identify gaps early.
