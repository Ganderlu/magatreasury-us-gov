Treasury Quarterly Refunding Webcasts

Webcasts are commonly used to provide public context on quarterly refunding plans and market conditions. This page serves as a destination for the Policy Issues menu link and explains what viewers typically learn from these events.

What Webcasts Typically Cover

  • Issuance overview: upcoming auction sizes and maturity mix.
  • Market conditions: interest rates, demand indicators, and liquidity topics.
  • Q&A themes: clarifications on issuance plans and rationale.

How to Use the Information

  • Cross-check: compare webcast messaging with published tables and statements.
  • Track changes: note how guidance evolves across quarters.
  • Learn terminology: refresh on auction mechanics and yield curve language.
