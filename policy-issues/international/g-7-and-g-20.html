G-7 and G-20

The G-7 and G-20 are multilateral forums where countries coordinate on global economic and financial issues. Discussions often cover growth, inflation, financial stability, development finance, and cross-border policy priorities.

Common Agenda Topics

  • Macroeconomic coordination: growth, inflation, and policy mix.
  • Financial stability: market functioning and systemic risk issues.
  • Development: debt sustainability and project finance coordination.
  • Tax and transparency: cross-border tax and financial integrity topics.
