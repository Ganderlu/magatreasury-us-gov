Multilateral Development Banks (MDBs)

Multilateral Development Banks provide financing and technical support for development and infrastructure projects. MDB discussions often focus on poverty reduction, economic growth, climate resilience, governance, and debt sustainability.

How MDB Financing Is Commonly Described

  • Loans and guarantees: support projects and reduce financing risk.
  • Grants: targeted support for specific objectives or low-income needs.
  • Policy support: reforms and institutional strengthening alongside funding.

Key Topics

  • Project selection: economic impact, feasibility, and safeguards.
  • Transparency: reporting, procurement standards, and accountability.
  • Debt sustainability: ensuring financing supports long-run stability.
