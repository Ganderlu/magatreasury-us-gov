Outbound Investment Program

Outbound investment policy discussions focus on how certain overseas investments may present national security concerns in specific contexts. Summaries typically emphasize scope, definitions, and compliance steps for covered transactions.

Common Elements

  • Covered activities: which investment types or structures are in scope.
  • Key definitions: how terms are defined for consistent application.
  • Reporting or restrictions: what actions are required for covered transactions.

How to Read a Program Summary

  • Scope: covered sectors, technologies, or transaction types.
  • Thresholds: criteria that trigger obligations.
  • Timing: effective dates and transition guidance.
