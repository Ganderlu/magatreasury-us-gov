Terrorism and Illicit Finance

Terrorism and illicit finance policy focuses on reducing the ability of bad actors to move and use funds through the financial system. Topics in this category include sanctions and related compliance concepts, anti-money laundering frameworks, and cooperation with domestic and international partners.
