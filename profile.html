Refund policy

Refund Policy (30 Days)

At Our Maga Shop, your satisfaction means everything to us. That’s why we proudly offer a 30-day refund policy on all eligible orders. Please review the details below:

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, contact us within 60 days so we can review and resolve the issue. Once received and inspected, we may issue a refund for the product cost. Please note: shipping fees are non-refundable.

Lost or Undelivered Packages

If your tracking shows “delivered” but you didn’t receive your package, please check with your local carrier and your delivery location first. If it’s still missing, contact us and we’ll assist with next steps.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by provider; allow several business days for the refund to appear after approval.

Return Conditions

For eligible returns, items must be unused and in the same condition you received them. Some items may be non-returnable for hygiene, safety, personalization, or clearance reasons.

Shipping

Shipping Policy

Our Maga Shop processes and ships orders as quickly as possible. Shipping times can vary based on your location, carrier conditions, and item availability.

Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 1–3 business days. During peak demand, promotions, or holidays, processing may take longer.

Shipping Time

Transit times depend on the carrier and your delivery address. Estimated delivery dates are not guaranteed and may change due to weather, carrier delays, or operational issues.

Tracking

When available, we provide a tracking number after your order ships. Tracking updates may take time to appear after the label is created.

Address Accuracy

Please make sure your shipping address is complete and correct. If an order is returned to us due to an incorrect address, you may be responsible for reshipping fees.

Lost, Delayed, or Missing Packages

If your package is delayed, please check the tracking updates first. If tracking shows “delivered” but you didn’t receive it, check with household members, neighbors, or the carrier. If it remains missing, contact us and we’ll assist with next steps.

Damaged in Transit

If your order arrives damaged, contact us as soon as possible and include photos of the item and packaging so we can help resolve the issue.
