Bonds and Securities

This page provides a plain-language guide to common Treasury bond and security topics and points you to the main public service hubs people use to buy, manage, and research Treasury securities.

What “Treasury Securities” Include

  • Treasury bills: short-term securities sold at a discount.
  • Treasury notes and bonds: coupon securities paying interest on a schedule.
  • Savings bonds: non-marketable securities held by individuals.

Common Things People Try to Do

  • Buy and manage Treasury securities in an account
  • Replace or research older savings bonds
  • Understand auction and yield information
