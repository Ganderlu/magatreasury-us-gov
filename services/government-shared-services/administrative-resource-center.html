Administrative Resource Center (ARC)

The Administrative Resource Center (ARC) is commonly referenced as a shared service provider. This page provides a plain-language overview of what “administrative shared services” generally include and how they’re used by organizations.

Typical Shared Service Capabilities

  • Financial operations support (including payment and accounting workflows)
  • Administrative support and process standardization
  • Reporting and operational performance tracking
  • Service management and customer support functions
