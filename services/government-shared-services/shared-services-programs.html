Shared Services Program (SSP)

Shared services programs standardize common business functions so multiple organizations can use consistent processes, tools, and service delivery models. This approach can improve reliability, reduce duplication, and make reporting and controls more consistent.

Why Shared Services Are Used

  • Consistency: common standards and definitions across organizations.
  • Efficiency: reduces repeated effort and duplicated systems.
  • Controls: strengthens financial and operational controls through standard processes.
  • Visibility: improves reporting, oversight, and performance tracking.
