Treasury Franchise Fund (TFF)

The Treasury Franchise Fund is commonly referenced as a vehicle that supports shared service operations. Shared services are used to deliver common administrative, financial, and operational capabilities across multiple organizations.

Examples of Shared Service Areas

  • Financial management support and reporting
  • Administrative support services
  • Technology and platform services for standard business functions
  • Customer support and service management
