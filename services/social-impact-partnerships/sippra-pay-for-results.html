Pay for Results (SIPPRA)

“Pay for results” financing structures tie funding to outcomes. This page provides a plain-language overview of how pay-for-results programs are typically described and what information is usually requested when evaluating an outcomes-based project.

Core Concepts

  • Outcome metrics: measurable results that define success.
  • Independent evaluation: objective verification of whether outcomes were achieved.
  • Performance payments: payments that occur when results meet the agreed threshold.
  • Risk sharing: incentives align around achieving results efficiently.

What a Strong Project Plan Usually Includes

  • Clear target population and baseline conditions
  • Implementation plan with roles, milestones, and risk mitigation
  • Data collection strategy and privacy protections
  • Budget assumptions and sustainability considerations
