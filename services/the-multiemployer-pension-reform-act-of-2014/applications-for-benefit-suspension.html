Applications

This page provides a plain-language outline of what an application packet usually includes and how to prepare materials so they’re complete and easy to review.

Common Application Components

  • Applicant identification, authorized contacts, and plan information
  • Supporting analysis, assumptions, and data used for calculations
  • Participant communications and notices
  • Records showing how requirements were met

Preparation Tips

  • Organize documents by section with clear filenames.
  • Provide a short summary describing what each attachment supports.
  • Double-check dates, counts, and definitions for consistency.
