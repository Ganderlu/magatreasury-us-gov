Auctions

Treasury auctions refer to the sale of Treasury securities and related auction activity. This page provides a plain-language overview of common auction terms and links to auction-related public resources.

Common Auction Terms

  • Announcement: details for the offering, including maturity and amount.
  • Auction: the process where bids are submitted and awards are determined.
  • Settlement: when payment and delivery occur.
  • Bid-to-cover: a simple measure of demand relative to amount offered.
